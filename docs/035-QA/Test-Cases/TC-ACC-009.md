# TC-ACC-009: Profit Margin Calculation

## Pre-conditions

- Logged in as Admin.
- Delivered orders with known revenue and profit.

## Test Steps

1. Open profit report or order profit detail.
2. Verify profit margin = (profit / revenue) * 100.

## Expected Result

- Profit margin matches the calculated percentage.
